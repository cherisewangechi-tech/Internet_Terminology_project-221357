What is Web 4.0?

Web 4.0 is envisioned as the "symbiotic" or "intelligent" web. This future stage involves a highly adnvanced level of interaction where humans and machines can communicate in real-time through constant connectivity.

It is closely linked to the Internet of Things and advanced AI, creating a aweb environment that is proactive and intuitive, potentially reacting to a user's needs before they are even explicitly stated.
