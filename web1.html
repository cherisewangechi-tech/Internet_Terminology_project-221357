What is Web 1.0?

Web 1.0 refers to the first stage of the World Wide Web's evolution, often descirbed as the "read only" web. During this era, websites consisted primarily of static HTML pages with very little user interaction or generated content.

Most internet users at this time were purely consumers of information and the web functioned like digital encyclopedia where businesses proviede data that individuals could view but no easily comment on or change.
