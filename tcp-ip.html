What is TCP/IP?

TCP/IP, which stands for Transmission Control Protocol and Internet Protocol, is a basic communication language of the internet. It acts as a set of rules that governs how data is sent from computer to another over a network.

While IP handles the addressing and routing of data packets to the correct destination, TCP ensures that the data is delevered reliably and in the correct order. Together, they form the "handshake" that keeps the entire internet cconnect and functional.
